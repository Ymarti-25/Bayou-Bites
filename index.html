Welcome to Bayou Bites!

Savour the Magic, Taste the Bayou!

Our Menu Book a Table

Welcome to Bayou Bites, where the magic of New Orleans meets the enchantment of Disney's The Princess and the Frog. Our story begins in the heart of the French Quarter, where jazz melodies float through the air, and the aroma of soulful cuisine beckons you.

Our Inspiration:

  • Inspired by the spirited Tiana and her culinary dreams, we've crafted a dining experience that celebrates tradition, creativity, and community. Our modern twist on classic bayou flavors pays homage to the rich tapestry of New Orleans culture.
  • Step inside our cozy restaurant, adorned with moss-draped chandeliers, wrought-iron accents, and vibrant murals that tell tales of bayou adventures. The air hums with the soulful tunes of live jazz bands, inviting you to sway and tap your feet.
  • Our kitchen is a magical cauldron where flavors collide. From gumbo to étouffée, jambalaya to beignets, every dish is a love letter to the bayou. Our chefs infuse each recipe with a dash of nostalgia and a pinch of innovation.

Signature Crab Cakes

Lump crab meat seasoned with Creole spices, pan-seared to perfection!

Savor the authentic taste of the bayou with our signature crab cakes. Each cake is brimming with succulent lump crab meat, delicately seasoned with a blend of Creole spices that capture the vibrant essence of New Orleans. Pan-seared to golden perfection, these crab cakes offer a crispy exterior that gives way to a tender, flavorful heart. Perfect as a starter or a main dish, they're a culinary journey to the heart of Southern hospitality.

NOLA Bread Pudding

Traditional New Orleans-style bread pudding with a bourbon sauce.

Indulge in the rich flavors of New Orleans with our classic NOLA Bread Pudding. This traditional dessert is a symphony of soft, custardy bread infused with the sweet, aromatic spices that New Orleans cuisine is famous for. Each bite is soaked in a decadent bourbon sauce, adding a warm, spirited note that elevates this comforting dish to new heights. It's the perfect finale to a vibrant bayou feast, embodying the soulful essence of the city's culinary heritage.

Voodoo Shrimp and Grits

Shrimp and grits with creamy stone-ground grits topped with sautéed shrimp in a spicy Creole sauce.

Step into a world of Southern charm with our Voodoo Shrimp and Grits. This dish features plump, juicy shrimp sautéed to perfection and nestled atop a bed of creamy, stone-ground grits. Each spoonful is enveloped in a rich, spicy Creole sauce that's been carefully crafted to invoke the mystique of New Orleans. It's a culinary spell that's sure to enchant your palate and leave you craving more. Dare to indulge in this bewitching bayou classic!

Cajun Spice Half Chicken

A succulent half chicken brushed with house-made chicory BBQ sauce, served with baked macaroni and cheese and coleslaw.

Experience the bold flavors of the South with our Cajun Spice Half Chicken. This dish features a succulent half chicken, generously brushed with our house-made chicory BBQ sauce that adds a smoky depth and a touch of sweetness. Served alongside our creamy baked macaroni and cheese and a crisp, tangy coleslaw, it's a hearty meal that perfectly balances spice, smoke, and soulful comfort. It's a tribute to the rich culinary traditions of New Orleans, sure to satisfy any appetite.

Muffuletta Sandwich

Stacked with mortadella, salami, rosemary ham, cheddar, provolone, and house-made olive relish on toasted New Orleans sesame seed bread.

Take a bite of New Orleans tradition with our Magnificent Muffuletta. This hearty sandwich is stacked high with layers of rich mortadella, spicy salami, and aromatic rosemary ham. Melting cheddar and smooth provolone cheese envelop the meats, creating a symphony of flavors. The sandwich is brought to life with our house-made olive relish, adding a tangy twist that dances on the palate. Served on toasted New Orleans sesame seed bread, each bite of this Muffuletta is a tribute to the vibrant spirit of the bayou. Perfect for a satisfying lunch or a casual dinner, it's a taste of the South you won't forget.
